Game Development Projects

Video game development is one of my biggest passions and one of the main reasons I became a software engineer.
It is hard. It involves so many different disciplines and skills. Programming, game design, art, animation, 3d modeling, sound design, music composing and more have to all come togheter to make a comprehensive work. However, I really like the challenge it poses.
Learning a little about each aspect of making a game has been very interesting and fulfilling. I may not be an expert on any of these areas alone, but I like being a jack of all trades kind of developer.

I don't expect to make a penny by making games nor I expect them to be played by many. I do game development as a hobby because I genuinly enjoy creating and learning, but still, I would really appreciate it if you checked out my games!
To this date, the only game I've released is Structured Defenders, which you can get for free from itch.io.
