Jane Doe's Condominium Manager


This application lets a condominium owner manage different aspects like lot ownership, area reservations and maintenance charges.
Co-Authored by Melanny Vargas, we made it for a programming course where we had to create a website with the .NET MVC framework and an SQL database with entity framework. Link to repository.

The users are shown a Home page where they can look at the condominium map, read recent messages written by the administrator and access other parts of the application.


Members of the community can make reservations of the different available areas. The system checks if the area is open and available at the given date and time.


The administrators can create different charges and fee plans for services like cleaning and assign them to the homeowners.
The homeowners can see a list of their monthly fees and confirm their payment.


The administrators can look at a dashboard of their monthly income from property charges.


Features for Homeowners:

  • List of their monthly fees and confirm payment.
  • Make reservations of different areas.
  • List of all their reservations.
  • Option to update orders' state.
  • Report incidents in the condominiums.

Features for Administrators:

  • Functionality to create fees and assign them to homeowners.
  • List of all fees and report of wich ones have been paid.
  • List of all reservations made by homeowners.
  • List of incidents reported by homeowners.
  • Functionality to assign lots and houses to homeowners.
  • Report of monthly income.

Security features:

  • Functionality to register and login users.
  • Encripted passwords.
  • User roles: Some pages can only be seen by users with an specific role.
  • Functionality to de-activate users by administrators.
